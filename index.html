Reference Modulation

Among all the generated outputs evaluated in our listening study, this example stands out as the most representative demonstration of our framework's capabilities. It is presented here as a reference point for the quality our system can achieve.

Autonomous Model — C major → F♯ minor (Track 190)
Commentary: The piece modulates from C major to F♯ minor; the transition occurs from 00:20 to 00:30. This example is particularly noteworthy because the model not only generates harmonically coherent and well-resolved voice leading, but also introduces rhythmic novelty within the transition window — making the modulation one of the most musically interesting outputs produced by our framework.
